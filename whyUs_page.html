About Us

Commandos Football Academy is an professional football academy, to create a positive learning environment in which players are encouraged to express themselves and play freely without fear, and to nurture young players so that they can achieve their full potential.

Located in Pune, the Academy aims to brings international coaching and training facilities through a curriculum prepared under the guidance of our Technical Director Mr.Rocky Arokiaswamy.We are committed to providing our students with the best possible training facilities available and we are currently training at the Race Sports Ground in NIBM. The grass ground and artificial grass grounds are perfect for training in any condition.
